QUESTION 2: Why must a SOC analyst NEVER use "Bridged" mode when detonating unknown malware?
ANSWER: Bridged mode gives the VM a real IP address on the same LAN as my home network. Malware detonated in this mode could scan, map, or attack other devices on the network. Host‑Only mode prevents this by isolating the VM from all external systems.
